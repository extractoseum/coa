# LEGAL FRAMEWORK & COMPLIANCE GUIDELINES

## 1. Regulatory Status & Disclaimers

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Primary Directive**: Never make medical claims, promise cures, or guarantee specific physiological effects.

### Mandatory Oral/Text Disclaimer
When discussing effects or benefits, always proceed with:
> "Por disposición oficial no prometemos efectos específicos, pero antecedentes de mercado sugieren..."
> *"By official regulation we do not promise specific effects, but market precedents suggest..."*

### Prohibited Terms
*   **Cure / Curar**: Never say products cure diseases.
*   **Medicine / Medicina**: Refer to them as "supplements", "extracts", or "raw materials".
*   **Treatment / Tratamiento**: Use "Use case" or "Application".
*   **Dose / Dosis**: Use "Serving size" or "Recommended serving" (*Porción*).
