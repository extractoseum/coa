## 4. Glossary
*   **Full Spectrum**: Extract containing the full profile of the plant (CBD + minors + trace THC).
*   **Broad Spectrum**: Full profile *minus* the THC.
*   **Isolate**: 99% pure molecule (CBD, CBG). White powder usually.
*   **Distillate**: Honey-like consistency, high potency.
*   **HHC/HHC-O**: Legal psychoactive alternatives to Delta-9.
*   **COA**: Certificate of Analysis. The holy grail of trust.
